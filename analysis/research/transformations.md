#####Yeo-Johnson Transformation
The Yeo-Johnson transformation extends the Box-Cox transformation to handle zero and negative values. Its formula introduces additional conditions to accommodate these scenarios, making it a more versatile choice for diverse datasets.

Key Features:
Handles zeros and negatives: Ideal for datasets with mixed numeric values.
More flexible: Applicable to a broader range of data types.
When to Use:
Your dataset includes zeros or negative numbers.
You need a transformation that requires minimal preprocessing.
